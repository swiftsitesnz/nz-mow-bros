Our Story

We're Jasper and Luke — two mates who started mowing lawns on Waiheke Island and quickly built a reputation for reliable, quality work. After growing our client base through word of mouth alone, we've expanded to Auckland's North Shore.

We're not a big corporate outfit with flashy gear and inflated prices. We're two hard-working local lads who show up on time, do a thorough job, and charge a fair price. That's it.

Most of our customers come through recommendations — and many have been with us since day one. We treat every property like it's our own, and every customer like a neighbour.
